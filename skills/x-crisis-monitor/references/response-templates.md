# Response Templates

Pre-approved response templates for different crisis levels and scenarios. Always adapt tone to match crisis severity and brand voice.

## Template Usage Guidelines

1. **Personalize all placeholders**: Replace `[brackets]` with specific information
2. **Maintain authenticity**: Use human-to-human tone, avoid robotic language
3. **Act quickly**: Acknowledgment within 2 hours, full response within 4 hours
4. **Verify facts**: Only respond with confirmed, accurate information
5. **Legal review**: Level 2-3 responses require legal approval
6. **Brand consistency**: Match existing brand voice and style

## Level 1: Customer Support Response

### Technical Issue - Individual User

**Quick Acknowledgment (< 2 hours):**
```
Hi @[user], thanks for flagging this issue with [product/feature]. We're looking into it right now. Can you DM us with more details? We'll get this sorted out quickly.
```

**Resolution Follow-up:**
```
Hey @[user], good news! We've fixed the issue you reported. Here's what happened: [brief explanation]. Your account is all set now. Thanks for helping us improve! ðŸ™
```

### Billing Issue - Individual User

```
Hi @[user], sorry to hear about the billing issue. We're checking your account right now. Can you DM us your email or account ID so we can resolve this immediately?
```

### General Complaint - Low Engagement

```
Hi @[user], thanks for sharing your feedback about [topic]. We want to make sure we address this properly. Can you share more details with us in DM? We take user experience seriously.
```

## Level 2: Operational Response

### Service Outage - Multiple Users Affected

**Initial Acknowledgment (< 2 hours):**
```
We're aware that some users are experiencing issues with [service/feature]. Our engineering team is actively investigating. We'll provide updates here as we learn more. Thank you for your patience.
```

**Status Update:**
```
Update on [service/feature] issue: We've identified the problem and are working on a fix. Estimated resolution time: [X] hours. We appreciate your understanding.
```

**Resolution Announcement:**
```
Good news: The [service/feature] issue has been resolved. Everything should be working normally now. If you're still experiencing problems, please let us know. Thanks for your patience! âœ…
```

### Influencer Complaint - Community Engagement

```
Hi @[influencer], thanks for bringing this to our attention. We hear your concerns about [issue] and take them seriously. Our team is reviewing this and we'd love to connect directly to discuss how we can improve. DM open.
```

### Product Change Controversy

```
We understand the concerns about [change]. Here's some context: [brief explanation of why]. We're actively listening to feedback and are open to adjustments. Share your thoughts with us here or DM.
```

### Pricing/Rollback Concerns

```
Regarding the recent changes to [pricing/feature], we understand this impacts our users. We're reviewing your feedback carefully. In the meantime, here's how existing customers are affected: [details]. We'll share updates soon.
```

## Level 3: Crisis Communication

### Data Security Breach

**Initial Statement (< 2 hours):**
```
We are investigating reports of a potential security issue affecting [scope]. We take this extremely seriously and are working with cybersecurity experts. We will provide updates as we have more information. Learn more at [link].
```

**Follow-up Statement:**
```
Update on security investigation: We have confirmed [details]. Here's what we're doing: [actions]. For affected users, we recommend [steps]. We apologize for this incident and will do better. More details: [link].
```

**Resolution & Remediation:**
```
We've completed our investigation of the [date] security incident. Here's what happened, what we're doing, and how we're protecting users going forward: [full postmortem link]. We are committed to transparency and security.
```

### Major Service Outage (Extended)

**Initial Statement:**
```
Critical: We are experiencing a major outage affecting [X% of users]. Our entire engineering team is working on this. We apologize for the disruption and will provide updates every 30 minutes here.
```

**Regular Updates (Every 30-60 mins):**
```
Update [X]: We're making progress on the outage. Our team has [specific progress]. We expect service to be restored within [timeframe]. Thank you for your patience.
```

**Post-Incident Communication:**
```
Service is fully restored. We know this outage was frustrating and we apologize. Here's what happened, what we did to fix it, and how we're preventing this in the future: [link to incident report].
```

### Regulatory/Legal Issues

```
We are aware of the allegations regarding [issue]. We are reviewing the matter carefully and will cooperate with any relevant authorities. We take our responsibilities seriously and will provide updates when we have more to share.
```

### Executive/Leadership Controversy

```
Regarding recent statements by [executive]: These views do not reflect our company's values or position. We take this matter seriously and are addressing it internally. We are committed to [core values]. We'll share more when we can.
```

## Crisis De-escalation

### When User is Extremely Upset

```
Hi @[user], I hear your frustration and I'm genuinely sorry this happened. I want to make this right for you. Can we connect in DM so I can personally help resolve this? We take this seriously.
```

### When Information is Unclear

```
We're looking into reports about [issue] and want to make sure we have all the facts before responding. We will share accurate information as soon as we can. Thank you for your patience.
```

### When Cannot Comment on Speculation

```
We've seen the speculation about [topic], but we don't comment on rumors. We'll share official updates when we have confirmed information. Please rely only on our official channels for accurate updates.
```
