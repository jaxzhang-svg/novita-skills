## [​](https://novita.ai/docs/guides/gpu-instance-quickstart-preparations\#preliminary-preparations)  Preliminary Preparations

Before deploying a new GPU instance, please be aware of the following points:

- First, create your Novita AI account and ensure you have enough credits in your account. Refer to our [Quickstart](https://novita.ai/docs/guides/quickstart) for more information.
- Confirm that you have read and understood the product introductions, pricing, features, usage restrictions, and terms of service for [Novita AI](https://novita.ai/).
- Understand the billing methods for GPU instances. Here’s some guidance: [GPU Instance Pricing](https://novita.ai/docs/guides/gpu-instance-pricing).
