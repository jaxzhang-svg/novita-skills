When you enable **“Automatic Top-Up”**, your account will automatically add credit if the balance falls below a specified threshold. Please follow the steps below to set up this feature:

### [​](https://novita.ai/docs/guides/auto-top-up\#1-enable-automatic-top-up)  1\. Enable Automatic Top-Up

Go to [Payment Methods](https://novita.ai/billing/payment-methods) in the console.

![](https://mintcdn.com/novitaai/pBY37YC9HPtF1YcS/guides/images/automatic-top-up-switch.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=pBY37YC9HPtF1YcS&q=85&s=2d9d485c491ec64f8328c06285528913)

If you don’t have a payment method, click **“Add payment method”**. You’ll be redirected to Stripe’s secure page to add one, and we do not store any payment information.

### [​](https://novita.ai/docs/guides/auto-top-up\#2-automatic-top-up-settings)  2\. Automatic Top-Up Settings

After you turn on the **“Automatic Payments”** switch, a configuration popup will appear automatically.Set valid values for **“When credit goes below”** and **“Bring credit back up to”**, then click **“Save settings”** to complete the process.

![](https://mintcdn.com/novitaai/pBY37YC9HPtF1YcS/guides/images/automatic-top-up-confirm.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=pBY37YC9HPtF1YcS&q=85&s=1bbb2e97b9461f571738d0002b95fe5f)
